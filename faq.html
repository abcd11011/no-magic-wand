Frequently asked questions

Straight answers on how we work, what you get, and what it costs.

  • Do you offer free discovery calls?

    No. Our thinking goes into the work itself. You can read our writing for free to decide whether we are a fit, and you can send a question before you buy. Every engagement begins with paid work.

  • Why do I pay upfront?

    Because the scope and price are fixed and clear before you buy. You know exactly what you are getting and what it costs. That lets us get straight to the work.

  • Do you do implementation?

    No. We deliver the strategy and the prioritized plan. Your team, or a freelancer, executes it. Every deliverable is built to be handed off: sequenced, specific, and ready to act on.

  • What happens after I get the plan?

    You execute it, or your team does. The plan is written so the next actions are clear and prioritized. The Growth Diagnostic also includes one optional walkthrough call to talk it through.

  • What exactly do I get with the Growth Diagnostic?

    A written full-funnel diagnostic covering acquisition, retention and unit economics, with everything sequenced into a prioritized 90-day plan. See the Work page for the full contents.

  • How long does it take?

    The Growth Diagnostic takes 10 to 14 working days from full access. The Brand and Growth Plan runs roughly 6 to 8 weeks across both phases.

  • What do you need from me?

    For the Growth Diagnostic: read access to your ad accounts, analytics, Shopify and email platform, plus your LTV, payback and margin numbers, your claims document, and your current KPIs. A short intake collects it all.

  • What is the guarantee?

    If the Growth Diagnostic does not surface at least 10 specific, prioritized opportunities to improve your growth, you get a full refund.

  • Who is the Growth Diagnostic for?

    Beauty and wellness DTC brands that are spending on growth and want a clear, expert read on what to fix and in what order.

  • How is the Brand and Growth Plan different from the Diagnostic?

    The Diagnostic is a fixed, full-funnel teardown with a 90-day plan, bought directly. The Brand and Growth Plan is a deeper, senior engagement for larger brands. It starts from what your brand stands for and builds a 12-month roadmap. It is by application and scoped to your brand.

  • Why is there no fixed price on the Brand and Growth Plan?

    Because the scope is built around your brand, so the exact figure is set during scoping. Engagements start from $12,000.

  • How does the application work?

    You apply using the form. If it is a fit, we invite you to a paid scoping session ($550, credited toward the work) to confirm fit and define scope. Then we begin.

  • Do you offer retainers or ongoing work?

    No. We work in fixed-scope projects. No retainers, no ongoing agency relationship.

  • Can I ask a question before I buy?

    Yes. There is a link on the Growth Diagnostic page to send a question. We keep it brief and async.

  • Do you only work with beauty and wellness brands?

    Yes. That focus is what makes the work sharp.

  • Who is behind No Magic Wand?

    A small senior team with deep DTC growth experience across beauty and wellness, including large-scale paid media and brand strategy. See the About page.
