What data is collected and why

Newsletter subscribers. If you sign up to the newsletter, your email address is collected and stored by our email newsletter provider (beehiiv or Substack). The legal basis for processing is your consent. You can withdraw consent and unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics. No data about your visit is collected or shared with advertising networks.
